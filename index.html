ABOUT MONI FIT

My name is Monika Wichan. I am a qualified personal trainer, fitness and wellness customer advisor, workout trainer, fitness instructor, fitness trainer, sport massage therapist and also an avid sportsman who began his adventure with sport with high-performance hip hop dance.
I completed my diploma in pedagogy and I have a master's degree.

I work with beginners as well as advanced people, amateurs and athletes.
I train one to one or group classes.

It is important to take this first step and get started then act. My job is to evaluate your options and encourage you to reach new goals or prepare you for a particular game.

I OPERATE INNOVATIVE. Combine knowledge about sport, training and healthy nutrition as well as pedagogical sciences. Empathically and individually - this is how I choose the scope of exercises and training content, monitoring the achievements of my clients on a regular basis, motivates and encourages me, but I also demand and care that your goal is achieved, because your goal is my goal.

Happy you - happy me!
